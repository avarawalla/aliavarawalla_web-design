transient collective

a residence for nomads

Toronto, Canada

Increased global mobility affords the opportunity for transitory lifestyles and shared economies, creating opportunities to transcend conventional boundaries and lead a nomadic way of life. Communities of location independent members can be formed as a counter reaction to permanence and create collectives of global residents. The Transient Collective is a residence and work space that offers members the ability to experience the city from a local perspective and be a part of a shifting, collaborative and interactive community. The program includes public spaces that create opportunities for locals and members to interact, providing a balanced working and living situation that gives them an authentic and involved connection to the city.
