an evolving office space

edinburgh, scotland

This design proposal creates a modern workspace for an innovative and growing technology company. The two large workspaces are separated by wooden perforated acoustic slats. These encapsulate the space for the developers who do more heads down, focus work, while the sales and marketing teams who are highly collaborative are in a more open environment. Modular furniture allows for adaptation to suit the changing requirements for this company, while biophilia is integrated throughout the space and in the form of wall partitions to promote a more welcoming and productive environment.
